# ELIG_MINIMUM_AGE (D1001)

## Description
The application is declined if the applicant does not meet the minimum legal age requirement.

---

## Evaluation Logic
applicant_age = integer (years)

---

## Threshold
applicant_age >= 18

---

## Outcome
- PASS: applicant_age >= 18  
- FAIL: applicant_age < 18  

---

## Typical Remediation
- Reapply once minimum age requirement is met

---

## Notes
Age is treated as a deterministic eligibility attribute with no exceptions.
